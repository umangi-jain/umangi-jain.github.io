Hi! I am a Pre-Doctoral Researcher at Google Research India in the Earth Observation Science team led by Dr. Varun Gulshan focusing on research situated at the intersection of computer vision and remote sensing. I work on multimodal representation learning and self-supervised learning to solve Geoscience research questions. I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology Madras with a B.Tech (Hons.) and a M.Tech in Electrical Engineering, and minors in Mathematics and Computing. I completed by thesis with Prof. Harish Guruprasad on generalization in deep neural networks. I also worked in the Computational Neuroscience Lab at IIT Madras. My research interests lie in applying machine learning for computer vision and multimodal content understanding.

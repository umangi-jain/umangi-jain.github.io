Hi! I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at University of Toronto, where I am advised by Igor Gilitschenski. I am currently working on neural rendering for 3D scenes. Previously, I was a Pre-Doctoral researcher at the Earth Observation Science group at Google Research, India where I worked with Varun Gulshan . My research there focused on self-supervised learning with large-scale multimodal satellite data for remote sensing applications.

I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology Madras with a B.Tech (Hons.) and a M.Tech in Electrical Engineering, and minors in Mathematics and Computing. I completed my thesis with Prof. Harish Guruprasad on generalization in deep neural networks. I also worked in the Computational Neuroscience Lab at IIT Madras. My research interests lie in applying machine learning for computer vision and multimodal content understanding.
